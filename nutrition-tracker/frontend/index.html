About Smart Nutrition Tracker

Your personal guide to healthy eating and fitness.

Welcome to the Smart Nutrition Tracker. Achieving your fitness goals isn't just about spending hours in the gym; it's about giving your body the right fuel.

This application is designed to help you stay on top of your daily nutrition goals effortlessly. Whether you are aiming to build muscle, lose weight, or simply maintain a healthy lifestyle, tracking your macros is the first step.

  • Home Tab: Log your meals, view your daily progress against macro targets, and manage your daily diary.
  • Ideal Meals: Get personalized, AI-driven meal suggestions based on your eating history and remaining macros for the day.
  • Analytics: Track your weekly average calorie intake and visualize your nutrient trends over time.
